# SteCute Design Context

## Users
Event attendees and hosts who use the app at weddings, parties, and events. They want to quickly capture fun photo strips, customize them, and save/share locally. Context is typically mobile phones in a social, celebratory setting.

## Brand Personality
**Playful & Fun, Warm, Approachable**

Three-word personality: Playful, Celebratory, Friendly

Emotional goals: Joy, delight, confidence — the interface should feel like a real photo booth at a party.

## Aesthetic Direction
- Visual tone: Soft, rounded, warm with vibrant pink accents
- Theme: Light mode primary
- Must maintain: Pink accent (#f45b8d), soft shadows, rounded corners, friendly feel
- Anti-references: Corporate, cold, minimal to the point of sterile

## Design Principles
1. **Celebration-first**: Every screen should feel fun and inviting, like being at a party
2. **Warm & approachable**: Soft colors, rounded shapes, friendly copy in Indonesian
3. **Quick & effortless**: Minimize steps, make actions obvious and delightful
4. **Offline-empowering**: Users feel confident their photos are private and always accessible
